Movie Details

Title: Casino Royale

Description: Casino Royale (2006) follows James Bond on his first mission as 007, where he must defeat a terrorist financier named Le Chiffre in a high-stakes poker game at Montenegro's Casino Royale. As Bond navigates deception, danger, and emotional entanglements, he begins to question the true cost of loyalty and love.
